About Me

Hi, I'm Nash!

I'm a UX writer and content designer passionate about creating digital experiences that feel natural and intuitive. With over 5 years of experience in the tech industry, I've helped companies of all sizes—from scrappy startups to Fortune 500 companies—communicate more clearly with their users.

My journey into UX writing started somewhat unexpectedly. I began my career as a traditional copywriter, crafting marketing campaigns and website copy. But I kept finding myself drawn to the smaller, more functional pieces of text—the button labels, error messages, and onboarding flows that actually guide users through experiences.

What I love most about UX writing is the detective work involved. Every project starts with understanding users' goals, fears, and mental models. I spend time researching user behavior, analyzing data, and collaborating closely with researchers and designers to ensure the words I write truly serve users' needs.

When I'm not optimizing microcopy or conducting content audits, you can find me reading about behavioral psychology, experimenting with new productivity tools, or hiking with my dog, Max. I believe that good content design comes from understanding people—and life outside of work gives me plenty of opportunities to observe how people really think and communicate.
